Pretty Pomegranate Salad

Description

Colorful springtime salad.

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces fresh baby spinach leaves
  • 10 ounces arugula leaves
  • Â½ red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 (10 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
  • 1 pomegranate, peeled and seeds separated

Steps

  1. Place the spinach and arugula leaves into a mixing bowl.
  2. Add the onion, mandarin oranges, and pomegranate seeds.
  3. Toss gently by hand to mix.
